# Business Insights

## Insight 1: Sales Trend

### Observation
Monthly sales remained relatively stable with periodic fluctuations throughout the analysis period.

### Data Evidence
Sales ranged approximately between 7M and 11M across months.

### Business Interpretation
The business experiences consistent demand with seasonal peaks and dips.

### Recommended Action
Investigate factors contributing to peak-performing months and replicate successful campaigns during lower sales periods.

---

## Insight 2: Regional Performance

### Observation
The South region generated the highest sales compared to other regions.

### Data Evidence
South region recorded sales of approximately 1.87M, outperforming East, West, and North regions.

### Business Interpretation
The South region is a major revenue contributor.

### Recommended Action
Increase marketing investment and inventory availability in the South region while analyzing strategies to improve lower-performing regions.

---

## Insight 3: Category Profitability

### Observation
Technology products such as Copiers and Phones generated higher profits, whereas some furniture sub-categories recorded losses.

### Data Evidence
Treemap analysis showed larger and greener blocks for Copiers and Phones.

### Business Interpretation
Technology products contribute significantly to overall profitability.

### Recommended Action
Focus promotional efforts on profitable product categories and review pricing strategies for loss-making categories.

---

## Insight 4: Customer Segment Behavior

### Observation
The Home Office segment generated the highest sales among customer segments.

### Data Evidence
Home Office sales were approximately 11.55M, slightly higher than Consumer and Corporate segments.

### Business Interpretation
Home Office customers represent a valuable customer segment.

### Recommended Action
Develop targeted campaigns and loyalty programs specifically for Home Office customers.

---

## Insight 5: Discount Impact

### Observation
Higher discount levels are associated with lower profitability.

### Data Evidence
Scatter plot analysis indicates that orders with discounts above 30% often generate lower or negative profits.

### Business Interpretation
Excessive discounting negatively affects business profitability.

### Recommended Action
Establish discount thresholds and monitor heavily discounted products to avoid profit erosion.

---

## Insight 6: Shipping Performance

### Observation
Standard Class shipping accounts for most delayed deliveries.

### Data Evidence
Shipping performance analysis showed a higher concentration of delayed orders within Standard Class shipping.

### Business Interpretation
Delivery inefficiencies in Standard Class may affect customer satisfaction.

### Recommended Action
Review logistics operations for Standard Class shipping and identify process bottlenecks.

---

## Insight 7: Return Pattern

### Observation
The Home Office segment in the South region exhibits the highest return rate.

### Data Evidence
Return Analysis highlight table shows the South–Home Office combination with a return rate of approximately 7.27%.

### Business Interpretation
Specific customer groups may be experiencing product quality or delivery issues.

### Recommended Action
Investigate product returns in this segment and identify root causes such as product defects or shipping damage.

---

## Insight 8: Business Risk and Opportunity

### Observation
A small number of categories contribute disproportionately to overall profit.

### Data Evidence
Treemap analysis shows significant profit concentration in a few sub-categories.

### Business Interpretation
Heavy dependence on a limited number of products increases business risk.

### Recommended Action
Diversify product offerings and improve profitability across underperforming categories to reduce dependence on a few high-performing products.
